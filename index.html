General Information & Getting a TAP Card
  • What is a TAP card?

    A TAP card is a reusable fare card used to pay fares on participating transit systems across the Los Angeles region.

  • How do I get a TAP card?

    Purchase a card online, at rail stations, or from TAP vendors throughout the region.

  • How long is a TAP card valid?

    Most TAP cards remain usable for ten years from the date they are issued.

Using TAP on Transit & Other Services
  • Where is TAP accepted?

    TAP is accepted on Metro and many other transit agencies across Los Angeles County.

  • How do I use TAP on buses and trains?

    Simply tap your card on the validator before boarding a bus or when entering a rail station.

  • Can I use TAP for bike share services?

    Yes. You can link your TAP account to Metro Bike Share to unlock and pay using Stored Value.

Managing Your TAP Card & Account
  • How do I check my balance?

    Check your balance through the TAP app, online at taptogo.net, or at any TAP vending machine.

  • How do I replace a lost card?

    Contact TAP customer service or visit a vendor. Registered cards can have remaining value transferred to a new card.

  • How do I update my card information?

    Sign in to your TAP account online or in the app to edit your profile and card details.

Adding Fare, Fare Products & Payment Options
  • How do I add fare to my TAP card?

    Load Stored Value or passes online, in the TAP app, at vending machines, or at retail vendors.

  • What payment methods can I use?

    TAP accepts major credit and debit cards, cash at vendors or machines, and eligible transit benefits.

  • What are the different fare products?

    Options include Stored Value, single-ride fares, day passes, and special program passes.

TAP Mobile App & Digital TAP Cards
  • Can I use my phone as a TAP card?

    With the TAP app, eligible phones can store a digital TAP card in Apple Wallet or Google Wallet for contactless use.

  • How do I manage my card in the TAP app?

    Sign in to the app to check balance, reload fare, and manage multiple cards.

  • Which phones are supported?

    Most recent iPhone and Android devices that support Apple Wallet or Google Wallet are compatible.
